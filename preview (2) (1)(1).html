Our Story

Who We Are

Kashyap Care is a grassroots NGO registered in Bengaluru, India. We work at the intersection of basic dignity and compassionate service — providing food, medicine, education, and livelihood support to underprivileged families across urban and rural communities.

We believe every person deserves a life of dignity. Our work is guided by Lalita Devi ji's quiet philosophy: give without counting, serve without expecting.

  • 💛
    Compassion first — every action rooted in empathy
  • 🤝
    Community-led — we work with, not just for
  • 🌿
    Sustainable impact — lasting change, not one-time relief
  • 🔍
    Full transparency — every rupee accounted for

What We Stand For

Our Four Pillars

We address the most fundamental needs of human dignity — food, health, education, and livelihood — through on-ground programmes rooted in the communities we serve.

🍚

Daily Essentials

Monthly distribution of food kits, clothing, hygiene supplies, and household necessities to families living below the poverty line.

🏥

Medical Aid

Free health camps, medicine distribution, ambulance assistance, and hospital access support for those who cannot afford healthcare.

📚

Education Support

School supplies, merit-cum-need scholarships, tutoring support, and digital literacy for children from underprivileged backgrounds.

🌿

Livelihood

Vocational skill training, micro-grants, and employment linkages to help families become self-reliant and financially independent.

What We Do

Active Programs

Four flagship programs, each designed to address a specific dimension of poverty with heart, consistency, and measurable impact.

01

Annapurna Ration Drive

Every month, we distribute dry ration kits — rice, dal, cooking oil, spices, and hygiene essentials — to over 100 families across Bengaluru. Named after the goddess of food and nourishment, this drive ensures no family sleeps hungry.

Food
02

Divya Swasthya Seva

Quarterly free health check-up camps with volunteer doctors, nurses, and specialists. We provide on-site consultations, blood tests, medicines, and referrals for serious conditions — bringing healthcare to those who cannot access it.

Health
03

Vanya Scholarship Fund

Named in honour of Vanya Kashyap, this annual scholarship supports girls from underprivileged families who demonstrate academic merit and potential. We cover school fees, books, uniforms, and examination costs for recipients.

Education
04

Lalita Warmth Drive

Every December, in loving memory of Smt. Lalita Devi, we distribute blankets, woolens, warm clothing, and winter essentials to the homeless and elderly who brave the cold alone. A small act of warmth in her name.

Winter
